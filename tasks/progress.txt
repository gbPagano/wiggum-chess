## Codebase Patterns
- PgnError: implement `std::error::Error`; anyhow's blanket `From<E>` covers it — do NOT add an explicit `impl From<PgnError> for anyhow::Error`
- SAN disambiguation: strip piece letter + 'x', take last 2 chars as dest, preceding chars are disambiguation (1 char = file or rank, 2 chars = full square)
- Board::make_move takes a ChessMove and returns a new Board (immutable move application)
- MoveGen::new_legal(board) is an iterator over all legal ChessMove values for the position
- Castling in UCI: e1g1 (kingside white), e1c1 (queenside white), e8g8/e8c8 for black
- Board::from_str(fen) parses FEN; fmt::Display on Board outputs FEN
